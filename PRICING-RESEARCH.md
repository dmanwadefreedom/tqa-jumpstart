## 4. PAYMENT PLAN STRUCTURES (INDUSTRY STANDARD)

Based on research across coaching and bootcamp programs:

### Standard Models:

**Pay In Full (PIF) with Discount:**
- 5-15% discount is standard for PIF
- Example: $2,997 PIF vs $3,291 on payment plan (10% premium on installments)
- Some programs offer up to $500-$3,000 off for PIF (Fullstack saves $3K, Evolve saves $500)

**3-Pay (Most Common for $2K-$5K Programs):**
- Total cost split into 3 equal or near-equal monthly payments
- Typically costs 5-10% more than PIF
- Example: $1,097 x 3 = $3,291 vs $2,997 PIF

**6-Pay (Common for $5K-$10K Programs):**
- Higher-priced programs use 6-pay
- 8-15% premium over PIF
- Example: $1,097 x 6 = $6,582 vs $5,997 PIF

**Extended Installments (10-12 Pay):**
- Clarusway: $969/mo x 10 months
- Makes the monthly number feel manageable
- Highest total cost due to extended premium

**Deposit + Deferred:**
- Fullstack: $500 deposit + defer remainder until employed
- Evolve: 2-part payment (half upfront, half at week 10)
- Springboard: Full payment or financing options

## 5. INCOME SHARE AGREEMENTS (ISAs) IN CYBERSECURITY

### Current State of ISAs (2025-2026):

ISAs have **declined significantly** in the cybersecurity training space. Key findings:

**Programs that still offer ISAs:**
- **Fullstack Academy:** $2,000 down payment, defer remainder until earning $35K+. Repay for 28 months after employment.
- **Springboard:** Job guarantee model (refund if no qualifying job in 6 months). Not a true ISA but similar risk-reversal.
- **Flatiron School:** ISA with cap around $17K. Repayment window 36 months.
- **Evolve Security:** Income-based repayment via Edly.

**Typical ISA terms:**
- Students pay 8-25% of post-program salary
- Minimum salary threshold: $35K-$50K before payments begin
- Payment window: 24-36 months
- Total cost often ends up 1.5-2x the upfront price

**Why ISAs are declining:**
- Regulatory scrutiny increased
- Default rates higher than expected
- Programs found that motivated, financially-committed students have better outcomes
- Some states now regulate ISAs as loans

### TQA Recommendation:

**Do NOT offer an ISA.** Instead, use a **results guarantee** structure:
- "If you don't land a cybersecurity role within 180 days of completing the program AND you completed all assignments, attended all calls, and applied to 50+ jobs -- we'll continue coaching you for free until you do."
- This is more powerful than an ISA because it shows confidence without the financial/legal complexity
- The conditional guarantee (must complete the work) protects against freeloaders
