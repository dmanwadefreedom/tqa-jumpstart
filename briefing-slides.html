What to skip

  • Generic bootcamp certificates
  • SANS/GIAC at $8,000+ (wait for employer)

Spend $1,200 on the right three. Not $8,000 on the wrong one.

The Interview Shift

"Tell me about a time you identified a security vulnerability."

Person A (Self-Taught)

"I found that our firewall rules were outdated and some ports were open. I updated the rules and closed the ports."

Screened out at round 2.

Person B (Trained)

"I identified 12 exposed ports beyond operational requirements. Assessed lateral movement risk, prioritized by severity, implemented updated ACLs, documented in the risk register, and recommended quarterly reviews."

Offer extended.

The difference is not experience. It is framing.
