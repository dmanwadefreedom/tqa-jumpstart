Trust Video — Record This Today
Action Required

60-second video sent to every person who registers. Goes out via WhatsApp the day after they pay. Film on your phone, horizontal, one take. Read the script once, then just say it in your own words.

Hey. You just grabbed a seat for the Career Briefing. Good move.

Most people in your position spend months looking at certifications, reading job posts, watching YouTube — trying to figure out the path on their own. You just skipped all of that.

Here's what you're walking into on April 18.

I've been hiring cybersecurity professionals for 28 years. Deloitte. EY. Shell. And the number one reason IT people stay stuck is not skill. It's how they position themselves. That's what I'm going to fix for you.

I'm going to pull up someone's LinkedIn live and show the room exactly what I look for when I'm on the hiring side. You'll see what gets you noticed — and what gets you skipped.

Block 11 AM Eastern on Saturday the 18th. Set a phone alarm right now while you're watching this. There's no recording — what happens in the room stays in the room.

You made a real move today. Most people just think about it. See you on the 18th.

Filming tips

  • Phone, horizontal, propped up. Natural light from a window if possible.
  • One take. Small mistakes are fine — over-produced feels corporate.
  • 60 seconds. If it's over 75, trim it. People are watching on their phone.
  • Talk to one person, not a crowd. Like you're telling a colleague what's happening Saturday.
  • End clean — "See you on the 18th" then stop. Don't trail off.
